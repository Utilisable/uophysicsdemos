A ball goes off on a tangent after being rolled around in an incomplete circular ring.

### Equipment ###
- Broken ring
- Metal Ball

### Instruction ###
Lightly roll the ball along the edge on the inside of the ring. Catch the ball slightly after it exits the ring.
